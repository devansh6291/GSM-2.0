About GS Motorsports

Driving Innovation, Racing Excellence

Our Mission

GS Motorsports is the premier motorsports club at our college, dedicated to fostering a passion for racing and automotive engineering. We provide students with hands-on experience in motorsports, from karting competitions to Formula SAE projects.

Our club combines the thrill of racing with cutting-edge engineering principles, creating an environment where students can develop technical skills, leadership abilities, and lifelong friendships united by a shared passion for speed.
